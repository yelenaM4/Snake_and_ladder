Snake And Ladders

An interactive online version of the well-known board game Snakes & Ladders is available. Children will enjoy rolling the dice and moving up the board in a race against the computer. Children will practice number recognition and counting from 1 to 100 as they travel along the numbered grids, which will help them with arithmetic abilities as well. Kids will have a ton of fun playing this captivating game. This game will help the child's counting, calculating, and mental acuity.
