Rules Of The Game



  • Each player puts their counter on the space.

  • Take it in turns to roll the dice.

  • you will have to get a six to start the game.

  • If your counter lands at the bottom of a ladder, you can move up to the top of the ladder.

  • If your counter lands on the head of a snake, you must slide down to the bottom of the snake.

  • The first player that reaches the highest space on the board, 100, wins the game. To win the player will need to roll the exact number to get to the last space.
